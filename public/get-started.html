Congrats, ZenUML is added to your Confluence!

Your team is more productive in creating and editing standard-compliant UML Diagrams.


How to add ZenUML on pages

ZenUML app lets you quickly add diagrams on your confluence pages.

How to edit ZenUML diagrams

You can edit the diagrams in editing mode by click the "Pen" icon. You can also edit it without leaving the view mode, provided your have the edit permision of the page.
